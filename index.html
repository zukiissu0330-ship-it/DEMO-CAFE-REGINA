Small Hunger

Also if you want to eat, you should go to Café Regina! For example, you can eat something here if you are feeling a little hungry. There is the farmer's board, filled with artisan cheeses, cold cuts and crispy freshly baked bread. At the weekend you will find many tasty Flemish classics.

Weekend Suggestion

During the weekend at Café Regina you can treat yourself to a delicious hot meal that is prepared with care and love. Our chef serves traditional Flemish classics that delight your taste buds. So be sure to drop by or contact this business.
